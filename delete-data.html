Delete Your Data

Request deletion of your Shedulytic account and personal data

We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from Shedulytic, please follow the instructions below.

Data That Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account information (username, email, password)
  • All tasks and reminders you have created
  • All habits and their associated settings
  • Habit completion history and verification records
  • Streak data and progress statistics
  • Location verification history (if applicable)
  • Pomodoro session records
  • All activity logs and usage data

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: Delete from Within the App

  1. Open the Shedulytic app
  2. Go to Profile section
  3. Tap on Settings
  4. Select Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion via email, please send an email with the following information:

  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request - Shedulytic"
  • Your registered email address
  • Your username (if known)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
